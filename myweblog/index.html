by kittyparot on September 13, 2022

I am Kitty, I am a cat. Cats are adorable animals and are petted by lots of people in the world. We are playful and spending time with them reduces stress and anxiety. We are omnivores. We eat vegetative items such as rice, milk, pulses, etc. as well as fish, meat, birds, mice, etc. Therefore, we can eat on both types of food. We are very good friends to humans if they trust us. We like to sleep close to them for their body warmth. We are so lovely and cute that's why many human love us.
